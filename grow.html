A/B testing

A/B testing is a way of presenting users two separate web pages to determine which one is better. For example, we can try different taglines on your homepage to determine which tagline potential customers understand more.

After we have collected enough data, you'll be able to tell which tagline your customers understand the most.

Improving little things like this can be the difference between you and Amazon, Facebook, or Google.
